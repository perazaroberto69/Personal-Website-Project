The Metro Series

The metro series is a series of 3 open world games that take place in russia after a nuclear war left most of the world a radioactive wasteland that is filled with mutant creatures that ypu have to watch out for, the series consists of Metro 2033, Metro Last Light, and Metro Exodus

I HIGHLY recommend playing this series as you get to follow the main character and see the devlopment of him and his friends through the series, the story is sometimes emotional and really action packed so if your into both of those things you should absolutley check it out.
